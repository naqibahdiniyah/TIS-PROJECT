# Overview 

Biocon is a biopharmaceutical company primarily based in Bangalore, India and are specialized in bioproducts, especially insulin production. It plays a crucial role in supplying high-quality healthcare products that comply international standards.

The visit was organized in order to give students real-world exposure on how technology and information systems impact biotechnology and pharmaceutical industry. 

The main goal of the visit was to provide students with exposure to real-world biotechnology and pharmaceutical operations in an industrial environment. 

Furthermore, the visit allows student to see the flow on how laboratory research is transformed into real products and being marketed globally.
